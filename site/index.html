Every closed gate is a COM1 marker on nested KVM. From leaving UEFI to a real Linux /init — one latch at a time.

  1. M1.0 ExitBootServices — own the machine after firmware
  2. M1.1 Real VMXON / VMXOFF

The invariant we will prove

For every valid EPT mapping from guest-physical to host-physical, that frame is exclusively owned by one guest — and belongs to neither the hypervisor nor any other guest.

Proven Core only. Everything else stays outside the verification budget — by design. Specs and proofs land incrementally; the first machine-checked invariant in CI is the credibility milestone.
