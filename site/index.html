The invariant we prove

For every valid EPT mapping from guest-physical to host-physical, that frame is exclusively owned by one guest — and belongs to neither the hypervisor nor any other guest.

L2 means Verus ghost specs and pre/post conditions are written (with runtime asserts + bounded Kani). L3 means cargo verus --verify machine-checks those specs. M3.14–M3.16 drafted, pinned, and linked the model. M3.17 discharged scoped exclusivity — 13 verified, 0 errors, no admit — ADR-006 L3 for the ghost model.
